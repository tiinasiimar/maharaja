Our Story

Maharaja is an authentic Indian restaurant located in Tallinn Old Town, offering the rich and flavorful traditions of Indian cuisine. Our experienced chefs prepare all dishes with the freshest ingredients, using traditional Indian spices and flavors.

We are dedicated to providing you with a genuine Indian dining experience in a comfortable and warm atmosphere. Our menu includes a wide selection of tandoori dishes, curries, biryanis, and much more, catering to both vegetarian and non-vegetarian palates.
