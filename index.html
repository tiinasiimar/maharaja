Our Story

In the heart of Tallinn lies Maharaja, a cozy and inviting restaurant that celebrates India's culinary heritage. This is not just another Indian restaurant; it is a pioneer, having opened its doors in 1989, being the first in the region to introduce the richness of Indian cuisine.

Maharaja is a dynamic blend of culture, traditions, and diverse culinary repertoire from different Indian states. We have made tremendous efforts to keep our recipes authentic, using the freshest spices and ingredients, along with traditional cooking methods to ensure the unique taste, aroma, and healthfulness of each dish.

We have a warm and hospitable atmosphere and strive to make our guests feel as if they are dining in India. "MAHARAJA" means "Great King" in Hindi. Here is your opportunity to be a king and offer your friends and family an Indian taste experience, as dining is a time for creating community and family.

We believe that your experience at Maharaja will be one you'll want to return to again and again. We invite you to visit and experience India's delicacies at our restaurant. Thank you for choosing us!
